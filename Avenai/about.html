Our Story

In 2012, four well-seasoned professionals decided to create a new, nimble and value-driven consulting firm. With decades of combined experience in business operations and management consulting, the four founding partners had gained a significant amount of knowledge in what a successful consulting company would comprise. They decided to create a consulting services firm that focused on all of the positives and factors that worked well. The goal was to build aunique organization with the right capabilities to deliver success to all of its clients. They named this organization, Avenai.

Today, Avenai is rapidly growing. Hiring the right professionals with the proper skill set and similar values has allowed for consistent growth. The vision and values that were fundamental building blocks of Avenai are still prominently displayed throughout the company. Strong leadership, close collaboration and always putting our clients first will continuously be key factors in ensuring the success of Avenai.

Our Culture

The way we think, interact, and work all stem from the values that are exhibited throughout our organization. Avenai’s culture is inclusive; valuing diversity and each employee’s unique qualities and opinions. We focus on thinking outside the box. Innovative ideas, discussions on strategy, and breaking away from the norms of our industry are all encouraged and supported. Flexibility is a key attribute that Avenai promotes.

Whether it’s flexible work arrangements, or changing roles to better suit your interests, we provide our employees with a strong work-life balance and chance to grow their careers. Avenai’s lattice organization approach offers employees an opportunity to learn first-hand from some of the most well-respected and experienced and well respected experts in the industry. No matter the position, all of our employees play a role in further developing Avenai as an industry leading organization.

Our Philosophy

With extensive industry knowledge, Avenai offers our clients a chance to grasp previously unattainable opportunities.

We believe that active listening, strategic thinking and close collaboration are key attributes to any successful project. Avenai develops comprehensive solutions that allow our clients to drive higher organizational performance. Our product is our people. By fostering an environment that encourages continuous learning, growth and innovation, we are able to achieve above and beyond our clients’ expectations. Acting with integrity and honesty is part of who we are. Avenai’s main focus it to exceed expectations and leave a positive impact that will have our clients wanting to build long-term relationships with us. When clients achieve their goals, we achieve ours. We are the avenue to innovation.

We’re not just good at business transformation

white coat challenge

Avenai has teamed up with White Coat Challenge team, which has collectively raised over $250,000 with the hopes of making a meaningful impact on cancer care in the Ottawa community. In 2012-2013, this team collectively raised funds to purchase new computer software for the CyberKnife. Avenai and its fellow team members also helped to raise funds to purchase a Capillary Sequencer for the new Molecular Oncology Diagnostics Lab.

sip n’ Style

Avenai is a proud sponsor of the Epilepsy Ottawa Carleton fundraising event “Sip N Style”. Avenai and other local companies teamed up together to sponsor the 2nd annual “Sip N Style” event. All proceeds from “Sip N Style” support the programs and services of Epilepsy Ottawa-Carleton (EOC), who are proudly celebrating 25 years of helping those living with seizures in Ottawa and surrounding communities.

eco equitable

Avenai is a proud sponsor of EcoEquitable, a registered Canadian charity, providing temporary employment and skills development training through small-scale textile recycling. In 2014 Avenai raised more than $2500 for EE, to be used to aid their mission of providing a bridge for immigrant and underemployed women to transition to sustainable fiscal independence, while reducing harmful waste in our communities.
